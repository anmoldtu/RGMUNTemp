WHAT IS

ROTARACT GLOBAL MUN 2018

SPECIAL EDITION




R

otaract Global Model United Nations 2018 is spread all over the world in our attempt to build a global youth decision making body that is able to respond to a wide range of problems that our planet is facing today and we will bring their voice on the desk of the United Nations, Rotary International, World Peace Forum and Toronto World Leadership Forum in order to build a future that the new generation wants: more just and more peaceful all around the world.
